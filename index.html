What is RISE?

“Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today” At Rancike, we believe that quality education must be affordable for everyone so that we are able to help learners achieve career goals and build a bright future.
A lot of talented aspirants who are good studies and want to achieve something academically often bump into the hurdles with regard to their financial backgrounds.
So we bring round you RISE, for the students of class 9 and 10. RISE is intended to spot such worthy students and provide them with the coaching and therefore the guidance they have to accomplish their dreams
RISE is a scholarship examination for class 9 and 10 students to encourage higher studies within the field of Science and other desired disciplines.
This exam identifies the tutorial potential of scholars to provide them 100% scholarship on the basis of their performance for Rancike.

Why should you take RISE?

  • RISE provides 100% scholarship on the course COURSE_NAME for the highest performers. The main motive of RISE is to provide financial assistance to worthy students. The scope of education is expanding nowadays, and so does the academic expenses. RISE will provide a 100% scholarship on the RANCIKE COURSE for the scholars of classes 9 and 10.
  • Receiving a merit-based scholarship indicates that you simply stood out among your peers. RISE will recognize this quality, and it's important that you just know to work out it too. While applying for colleges or jobs in the future, you have got proof that in a minimum of one area you'll be able to distinguish yourself from other peers
  • Students get to benchmark against the best brains across India to assess their potential
  • You can showcase your rank and results on your resume. Distinguishing your resume from those sent by many other applicants may be difficult. RISE can make it easy to create your resume stand out. The candidates who are rewarded with any scholarship in the past are preferred over other candidates as they are considered hard-working and knowledgeable.
  • RISE will also give you an opportunity to meet new intelligent people and learn and grow with them. This will help you with other academic opportunities, research opportunities, and will give you a close-knit friendship group in the same academic field.
